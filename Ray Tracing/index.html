Ray Trace Simulator

This ray tracer has been one of the most exciting and challenging projects I’ve worked on. It creates realistic images by simulating how light interacts with objects, bringing vivid and dynamic visuals to life. Seeing my code produce something so lifelike felt almost magical and showed me just how incredible it is to combine math, physics, and programming.

Working on this pushed me to solve problems I’d never encountered before, like debugging reflections and fine-tuning the camera system. It taught me how to break down complex challenges and made me a more patient and creative programmer. This project wasn’t just about challenging myself, it deepened my passion for coding and what’s possible with it.
