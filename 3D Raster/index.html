3D Rasterizer

3D always felt just out of reach for me creatively. This is my fifth or sixth attempt at a 3D renderer, but after taking my first Linear Algebra class at ASU, everything finally made sense. What used to feel overwhelming started to click, and breaking the project into smaller tasks made it much more manageable.

I built a fully functional camera system with movement, rotation, and zoom, along with basic lighting to add depth. The renderer supports .obj and .tri file uploads, making it easy to view external 3D models. Right now, it only works on desktop sorry, mobile users but it’s been one of the most rewarding projects I’ve built.
